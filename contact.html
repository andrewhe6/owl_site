About The Site

The goal of this website is to teach people about the different types of owls living within the Hawaiian Islands. Although there are currently only 2 species in the islands, these owls are still important to the ecosystem and the culture of the people. By harboring greater knowledge of Hawai'i's fauna, we can aim to reduce the increasing effects of biodiversity loss and protect the diverse organisms found within the islands.
